Award Points
Go from Your Currency -> Galaxy Points
Backend required: This is for your security - it prevents malicious actors from sniffing the HTTP request and crediting themselves with unlimited points (charging you in the process). You can award points to users without a backend in the following ways:
  • Referral rewards
  • Prizes for the top scorers
This is set up in your dashboard.
Giving points is a great way to add value to your currency and give users a reason to play. Players can use credits to purchase cosmetic items that appear on the leaderboard. You will be charged 1 cent per point. You can choose any exchange rate from your currency to points that you'd like.

For example, you can offer the ability for players to convert 100 coins into 100 credits, which players can use to buy super-cool sunglasses. You'd be charged $1 for this transaction. You could 100 coins for a $1.99 in app purchase and earn a profit, or boost engagement by making players earn coins in game.
